Study Guide

The heart of the program is Dr. Arthur’s Study Guide for the NCE, which is a user-friendly publication designed to prepare you for the exam. The Study Guide is updated regularly by Dr. Arthur – often multiple times per year.

Covers All 8 CACREP Areas

The Study Guide is organized according to the 8 CACREP areas: Professional Orientation; Research and Program Evaluation; Appraisal; Lifestyle and Career Development; Helping Relationships; Group Counseling; Human Growth and Development; Social and Cultural Foundations. Example questions reflect those objectives. This is significant because the NCE is developed from CACREP programs.
