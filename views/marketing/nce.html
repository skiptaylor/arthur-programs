NCE Study Guide

Comprehensive and user friendly, this is the study guide that has been instrumental in helping hundreds of counselors pass the NCE. It will help you assess your weaknesses and strengthen what you know. Included are 2 exams, each with 200 questions and detailed answers that give insight into the style and format of the NCE's questions. Available in hard copy or eBook along with 2 online exams - one Pre-Assessment Exam and one Practice Exam - it covers all eight (8) CACREP Content Areas. Also available for an additional cost are two additional 200 question online exams.

Covers all eight (8) CACREP Content Areas

  • Professional Orientation
  • Research and Program Evaluation
  • Appraisal
  • Lifestyle and Career Development
  • Helping Relationships
  • Group Counseling
  • Human Growth and Development
  • Social and Cultural Foundations

Dr. Arthur's expertise

Dr. Gary Arthur, Ed.D., LPC, NCC, Professor Emeritus of Professional Counseling at Georgia State University, is personally available to answer any questions you may have about the study guide or the exam (up to 30 minutes). He has taught counseling courses at the graduate level for 34+ years and he was named Counselor Educator of the Year 2007 by the Licensed Professional Counselor Association of Georgia. He has published numerous articles in referred journals, and wrote this study guide for the specific purpose of preparing you for the NCE licensure exam.

Money Back Pass Guarantee Policy Terms

CTC offers a money back pass guarantee for the Dr. Arthur’s Study Guide. Pass the NCE, or CTC will refund your money for the cost of the Study Guide (excludes shipping and any sales tax).
